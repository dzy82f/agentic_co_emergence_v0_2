# MANIFESTO

## Why this project exists

Agentic Co-Emergence is an exploration of a simple but ambitious idea:

> Collective understanding may be treated as a computational object.

The project is not primarily about creating agents that converse. It is
about understanding how independent perspectives become shared
understanding through governed interaction.

## Our Position

We believe that:

-   discussion is a process, not a product;
-   understanding is more important than transcript;
-   governance shapes reasoning without prescribing conclusions;
-   constraints can increase rather than diminish creativity;
-   every conclusion should be traceable to its history.

## What we are building

We are building an operational envelope within which collective
reasoning can be observed, analysed and improved.

The runtime governs the process of discussion. It does not dictate the
content of thought.

## Beyond AI

Although the first implementations use language models, the ideas are
intended to apply equally to human groups and hybrid communities.

The underlying question is independent of today's models:

How does understanding emerge?

## Success

The project succeeds if it provides a rigorous, transparent and reusable
framework for studying the evolution of collective understanding.
