# Research Paper 0005
# How Dialogue Transforms Understanding

**Version:** 0.1  
**Status:** Working Paper  
**Milestone:** 0.3

## Purpose

This paper explains how dialogue transforms Understanding States.

## Core Proposition

Dialogue is a sequence of operators acting upon Collective Understanding.

Each conversational move has the potential to produce an Understanding Delta.

## Candidate Dialogue Operations

- Introduce evidence
- Challenge an assumption
- Connect ideas
- Distinguish concepts
- Expose contradiction
- Propose synthesis
- Clarify uncertainty
- Recommend action

## Computational Interpretation

Understanding State

↓

Dialogue Operation

↓

Understanding Delta

↓

Understanding State

## Acceptance Criteria

Each discussion transition can be interpreted as one or more dialogue operations producing an Understanding Delta.

## Open Questions

- Are dialogue operations universal?
- Can they be composed?
- Which operations produce the greatest epistemic benefit?
