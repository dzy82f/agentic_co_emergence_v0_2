# Research Paper 0002
# What Is an Understanding Delta?

**Version:** 0.1  
**Status:** Working Paper  
**Milestone:** 0.3

## Purpose

Research Paper 0001 proposed that understanding should be viewed as an evolving computational state.

This paper asks:

> **What is the difference between two understanding states?**

We refer to that difference as an **Understanding Delta**.

## Research Question

What constitutes a meaningful change in understanding?

The objective is not to measure how much discussion has occurred, but to identify how the system's orientation toward the issue has changed.

## Working Definition

An **Understanding Delta** is the smallest meaningful description of the change between two immutable Understanding States.

It captures *what changed*, rather than *what exists*.

## Candidate Delta Types

- New claim introduced
- Existing claim refined
- Claim rejected
- New distinction recognised
- New relationship discovered
- Hidden assumption exposed
- Contradiction identified
- Contradiction resolved
- Confidence increased
- Confidence reduced
- Uncertainty clarified
- New uncertainty discovered
- Issue reframed
- Consequence recognised
- Action implication changed

## Desired Properties

An Understanding Delta should be:

- Immutable
- Explainable
- Composable
- Comparable
- Independent of implementation

## Acceptance Criteria

Given two discussion states, the system can identify and explain the conceptual changes that occurred between them.

## Open Questions

- Are all delta types equally important?
- Can deltas be composed?
- Can multiple small deltas produce emergent conceptual shifts?
- Are some deltas irreversible?
