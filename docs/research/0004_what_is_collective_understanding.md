# Research Paper 0004
# What Is Collective Understanding?

**Version:** 0.1  
**Status:** Working Paper  
**Milestone:** 0.3

## Purpose

Individual agents possess perspectives.

This paper asks whether understanding can belong to the discussion itself.

## Hypothesis

Collective Understanding is an emergent property of structured interaction.

It is not:

- the transcript
- the average opinion
- unanimous agreement
- the most knowledgeable participant

It is the evolving epistemic state of the discussion.

## Characteristics

- Shared concepts
- Recognised disagreements
- Distributed expertise
- Emerging synthesis
- Unresolved tensions
- Coordinated action possibilities

Agreement is optional.

## Acceptance Criteria

The computational model distinguishes changes to individual perspectives from changes to Collective Understanding.

## Open Questions

- Can Collective Understanding regress?
- How is forgotten knowledge represented?
- Can collective insight emerge that no individual possessed?
